What Is Drift?

Drift is the spontaneous deviation from structure, direction, quality or architecture. It emerges naturally in any system that lacks ecosystemic stability.

Drift is not caused by teams, not the result of bad intent, and not a process failure. Drift is a natural law in complex IT landscapes.

  • Architectural drift — structure shifts
  • Process drift — agreements fade
  • Dependency drift — relationships change
  • Identity drift — roles and boundaries blur
