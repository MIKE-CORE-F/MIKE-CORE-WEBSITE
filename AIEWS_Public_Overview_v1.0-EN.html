AIEWS • Public Overview

AIEWS is the AI‑supervision layer within the MIKE‑CORE ecosystem. It monitors context, drift, stability and audit‑trails across all layers.

Purpose of AIEWS

The purpose of AIEWS is not to replace people, but to protect them: from chaotic growth, invisible drift and unconscious violations of doctrine.

Public Scope
  • Transparency: clarifying what AIEWS does and does not do.
  • Stability: helping prevent IT environments from collapsing under pressure.
  • Drift Control: detecting and correcting deviations.
  • Audit Trails: keeping decisions and changes traceable.
Relation to MIKE‑CORE

AIEWS is designed as a protective layer around MIKE‑CORE: it guards doctrine, structure and ecosystemic boundaries, ensuring that expansions never break the core.
