AIEWS • Public Overview

AIEWS is the AI‑supervision layer within the MIKE‑CORE ecosystem. It monitors context, drift, stability and audit trails across all layers, ensuring that technicians and architects can build safely without breaking core principles.

Purpose of AIEWS

The purpose of AIEWS is not to replace humans, but to protect them: from chaotic growth, invisible drift and unintentional violations of doctrine.

Public Scope

  • Transparency: clarifying what AIEWS does and does not do.
  • Stability: helping prevent IT environments from collapsing under pressure.
  • Drift Control: detecting deviations and guiding systems back to the core.
  • Audit Trails: keeping decisions and changes traceable.

Relation to MIKE‑CORE

AIEWS is designed as a protective layer around MIKE‑CORE: it guards doctrine, structure and ecosystem boundaries, ensuring that expansions never break the core.
