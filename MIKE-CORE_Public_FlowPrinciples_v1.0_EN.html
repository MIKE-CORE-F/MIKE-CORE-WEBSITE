What are ecosystemic flows?

Ecosystemic flows describe how information, decisions and movement travel through the MIKE‑CORE ecosystem. They are not linear, not team‑dependent, and not bound to frameworks.

An ecosystemic flow:

  • moves through the landscape without breaking dependencies
  • detects drift naturally
  • keeps coherence intact
  • is reproducible in any context
  • is not sensitive to framework failures
