Frequently Asked Questions
1. Who is MIKE‑CORE training for?
For youth without diplomas, professionals who are stuck, vulnerable groups and anyone who wants to build stability.
2. Do I need technical knowledge?
No. MIKE‑CORE works with reproducible steps and ecosystemic guidance — no prior knowledge required.
3. What makes MIKE‑CORE different from classic training?
MIKE‑CORE focuses on stability, structure and ecosystemic thinking. No pressure, no chaos, no throw‑away mindset.
4. How long does a training pathway take?
Pathways are modular and scalable. They range from short workshops to long‑term guidance.
5. Can my organisation collaborate with MIKE‑CORE?
Yes. Schools, youth work, local authorities and social organisations can start ecosystemic projects.
6. Is there a cost to participate?
Public pathways are low‑threshold. For organisations, ecosystemic collaboration models are used.
7. What happens to repaired hardware?
Repaired hardware is used for inclusion projects, training or redistributed to vulnerable groups.
8. How is stability measured?
Through reproducible steps, drift recognition, ecosystemic analysis and audit‑proof documentation.
