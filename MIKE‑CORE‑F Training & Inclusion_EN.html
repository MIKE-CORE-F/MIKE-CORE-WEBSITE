Training & Inclusion

A socio‑technical ecosystem designed to build stability for youth, professionals and vulnerable groups.

Why this page exists

MIKE‑CORE‑F is more than an IT ecosystem. It is a structure that protects people, creates stability and opens opportunities for everyone — regardless of diploma, background or starting position.

1. Training pathways for youth without a diploma

Many young people drop out because traditional education relies on abstract theory. MIKE‑CORE‑F provides reproducible steps, ecosystemic guidance and a safe learning environment.

  • clear diagnostic structures
  • step‑by‑step stability building
  • ecosystemic guidance
  • growth without diploma barriers
2. For professionals who are stuck

A diploma does not guarantee stability. MIKE‑CORE‑F supports professionals who experience noise, pressure or inconsistency.

  • restoring stability
  • drift detection
  • ecosystemic analysis
  • audit‑proof workflows
3. Inclusion for vulnerable groups

MIKE‑CORE‑F protects people who are written off by traditional systems. Stability is the foundation on which everyone can grow.

  • low‑threshold support
  • ecosystemic safety
  • reproducible steps
  • growth for everyone
Anti‑Waste Culture

MIKE‑CORE‑F combats waste culture on three levels: people, hardware and knowledge.

  • People: no one is discarded because of mistakes or background.
  • Hardware: diagnosis, repair and reuse.
  • Knowledge: nothing disappears — everything becomes reproducible.
