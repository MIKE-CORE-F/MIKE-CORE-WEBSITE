MIKE‑CORE Ecosystem Overview

A clear overview of the complete MIKE‑CORE ecosystem.

TRIAD — Educational Core

TRIAD forms the foundation of the MIKE‑CORE ecosystem. It is designed to teach young people, students and technicians‑in‑training a safe, understandable and reproducible way of thinking.

  • TRIAD 1.0 Basic — Entry‑level license for youth and education
  • TRIAD 1.0 Professional — For advanced learners and practical training

T‑Licenses — Business Stability

The T‑licenses are designed for organizations that require stability, diagnostics and reproducible workflows in their IT environment.

  • T1 Basic — Business entry‑level license
  • T2 Professional — Deeper diagnostics and stability
  • T3 Master — Ecosystem architecture and strategic stability

AIEWS — Advanced Intelligence & Ecosystem Workflow System

AIEWS is the advanced layer within MIKE‑CORE. It is not publicly available, but it powers ecosystem thinking, stability analysis and reproducible workflows.

  • AIEWS Demo Dashboard
  • AIEWS Lite Viewer
  • Full Ecosystem Engine (not public)

Ecosystem Philosophy

  • Stability over speed
  • Reproducibility over improvisation
  • Audit‑proof over trial‑and‑error
  • Structure over chaos
  • Ecosystem thinking over isolated tools
