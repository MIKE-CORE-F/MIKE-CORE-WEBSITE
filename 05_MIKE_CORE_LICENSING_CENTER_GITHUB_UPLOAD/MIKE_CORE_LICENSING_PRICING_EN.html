Overview of the public license levels and their positioning.

Why Pricing v1.1?

This version provides a clearer overview of the different license levels within the MIKE‑CORE ecosystem. The goal is transparency, simplicity and a professional presentation for organizations, schools and partners.

Educational Licenses

  • TRIAD 1.0 Basic — Entry‑level license for youth and educational programs
  • TRIAD 1.0 Professional — For advanced learners and practice‑oriented training

Educational licenses are designed to be safe, understandable and reproducible without technical overload.

Business Licenses

  • T1 Basic — Business entry‑level license for stability and safe workflows
  • T2 Professional — Professional level with deeper diagnostics and stability
  • T3 Master — High‑level ecosystem architecture and strategic stability

Business licenses focus on reproducible workflows, stability and risk analysis.

Pricing Structure

The exact prices are determined based on:

  • the type of organization
  • the number of users
  • the required support level
  • the complexity of the environment

For official quotes or partnerships, please contact the MIKE‑CORE communication channels.
