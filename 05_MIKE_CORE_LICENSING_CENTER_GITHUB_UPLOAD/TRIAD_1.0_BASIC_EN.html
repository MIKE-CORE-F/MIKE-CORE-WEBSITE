TRIAD 1.0 — Basic

The entry‑level license for youth, beginners and educational programs.

What is TRIAD 1.0 Basic?

TRIAD 1.0 Basic is an educational license that gives young people, students and beginners a clear and safe introduction to the MIKE‑CORE ecosystem. It is designed to make complex IT concepts understandable without technical overload.

Who is this license for?

  • Young people who want to learn how a computer really works
  • VDAB introductory training programs
  • Schools and educational institutions
  • Beginners who want a safe and solid foundation

What do you learn in TRIAD Basic?

  • How a system remains stable
  • The most important hardware components
  • How to recognize problems without tools
  • How to work safely without risks
  • The fundamentals of TRIAD thinking

Next step

After TRIAD 1.0 Basic, you can advance to TRIAD 1.0 Professional, where you dive deeper into diagnostics, stability and real‑world practice cases.
