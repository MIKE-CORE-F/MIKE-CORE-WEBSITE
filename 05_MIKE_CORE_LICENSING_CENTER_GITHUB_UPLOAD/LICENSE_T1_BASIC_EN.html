MIKE‑CORE T1 — Basic License

The business entry‑level license for stable, safe and reproducible IT environments.

What is the T1 Basic License?

The T1 Basic License is the first level within the business TRIAD licenses. This license is designed for organizations that want to establish a reliable foundation for diagnostics, stability and operational safety.

Who is T1 intended for?

  • Small businesses that want to stabilize their IT environment
  • Start‑ups seeking a safe technical foundation
  • IT teams that require a reproducible workflow
  • Organizations that want to introduce TRIAD thinking

What does T1 Basic include?

  • Introduction to TRIAD diagnostics
  • Basic principles of stability and risk analysis
  • Fundamental operational workflows
  • Safe and reproducible IT procedures

Next step

After T1 Basic, an organization can advance to T2 Professional, where deeper diagnostics, stability analysis and operational workflows become central.
