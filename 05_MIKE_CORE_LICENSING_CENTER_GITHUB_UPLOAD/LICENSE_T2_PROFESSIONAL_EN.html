What is the T2 Professional License?

The T2 Professional License is designed for organizations that want to go beyond basic stability. T2 focuses on deeper diagnostics, reproducible workflows and understanding system behaviour under pressure, load and fault conditions.

Who is T2 intended for?

  • IT teams responsible for operational stability
  • Companies that want to standardize diagnostic processes
  • Technicians who want to apply TRIAD thinking professionally
  • Organizations aiming to minimize risks through reproducible workflows

What does T2 Professional include?

  • Advanced TRIAD diagnostic methods
  • Analysis of system behaviour under load
  • Operational stability procedures
  • Risk assessment based on behaviour and patterns
  • Reproducible workflows for teams
