This visual map is intentionally limited to the public layer. It shows:

  • the position of AIEWS within the ecosystem
  • its relationship to TRIAD, HEXAD, Cockpit and MIKE‑CORE Elite

It does not show:

  • kernel logic or internal runtime
  • blueprint architecture or validation chains
  • internal domain rules or module architecture

The map is designed as a public reference: suitable for talks, documentation, education and audits — without granting access to the private core of MIKE‑CORE or AIEWS.
